What's this all about?

We're bringing hackathons to UP interns! Come join us on Saturday, July 26 at 10:00 am for 24 hours of coding!

Meals and snacks will be provided as well as a few chances to win some door prizes! Projects will be scored and winners will be announced on Sunday morning.

Tips

You may want to look into some web/mobile technologies to help familiarize your self with technologies that can be used for the hackathon.

  • Mobile development platforms such as Phonegap, Android, or iOS.
  • Google Map API or any other map APIs
  • Web development platforms such as Xampp, Wamp, Lamp, NodeJS... etc to help you get your dev environment up and running fast
  • Databases such as sql, mysql, nosql, etc.
  • APIs for mobile devices (sensor technology like accelerometer or GPS)

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What is this UP Hackathon?
    A: This is a programming competition! You will have 24 hours to work with a team to create a solution to certain business problems.

    Q: You mentioned this Hackathon was primarily geared towards web/mobile development. What if I'm not comfortable with either?
    A: While we would like to see web/mobile development, you are also welcome to work with hardware or create a backend application!

    Q: Can I still win this competition if I decide to create a hardware or backend solution?
    A: Of course! You are not required to create a web/mobile solution to win.

    Q: What type of hardware will be available?
    A: We can provide your group with a Raspberry Pi, a cell modem and various sensors.

    Q: What if I already have an Arduino/ other hardware and want to use this instead?
    A: Please feel free to bring this with you!

    Q: Who is eligible to participate?
    A: You must currently be an IT intern with UP.

    Q: How many people can I have in a team?
    A: Each team may consist of up to 3 people max.

    Q: Can I choose to work alone?
    A: Of course you can! However, with 24 hours this would not be advised.

    Q: What if I want to compete but can't find a group?
    A: We can pair you up with others at the event.

    Q: What problems will we be asked to solve?
    A: We will be providing business problems related to our theme 'Courage to Care' at the start of the event.

    Q: What are the prizes again?
    A: Every individual on the winning teams will receive the following prizes:

    - 1st Place: Macbook Airs
    - 2nd Place: iPads
    - 3rd Place: $100 Amazon giftcards

    There will also be random prizes throughout the night just for attending.

    Q: What if I'm not confident with my programming abilities?
    A: Even more reason to go! The goal of the Hackathon is to get you coding and explore new technology! We are expecting a variety of skill levels and competencies, and will judge according. At the very least you'll get free food, a T-shirt, and a chance to win prizes! There's no reason not to come!

    Q: 24 hours is a long time commitment. What if I don't want to stay up all night?
    A: You are welcome to bring a sleeping bag or go home to take a nap.

    Q: Can I bring my own computer to the event?
    A: Yes! Not only do we allow it, we recommend it. It would be best if you for you to have your programming environment set up in advanced.

    Q: What if I don't have a computer to bring?
    A: Let us know and we'll provide you with a laptop to borrow.

    Q. How should I prepare?
    A. On the website, the section "Tips" outlines some technologies that would be interesting to use in your project. It is highly recommended to analyze safety in the railroad and where it can be improved upon.

    Q. Can I bring my own snacks or drinks?
    A. Snacks and non-alcoholic beverages only, please. UP has a strict no-alcohol policy. Do not come into the UPC building if you have consumed alcohol!
